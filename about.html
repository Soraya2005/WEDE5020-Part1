From Passion to Pastry

The Journey of Emma Stone

Emma Cape Town is the lifelong dream of its founder, Emma Stone. A self-taught pastry chef, Emma her journey in her home kitchen, driven by a passion for transforming simple, high-quality ingredients into edible art. Her creations, shared on social media, quickly garnered a following of connoisseurs who appreciated her blend of classic technique and modern creativity.

Today, her first physical boutique is a testament to that journey—a place where craftsmanship, flavour, and elegance meet.

Our Mission

To elevate pastry to an art form, creating visually stunning and unforgettably delicious experiences. We are committed to using the best local and seasonal ingredients, with meticulous attention to every detail.
